Balloons

Balloons are rubber sock thingies that inflate once you blow air into them. To inflate means to increase in size because of air.
You can get them for very cheap at your local dollar store, or buy them anywhere else.

Powerade

Powerade is an energy drink, similar to Gatorade (no copyright please) that has a lot of electrolytes in it. Powerade comes in mainly two flavors, Mountain Berry Blast and Fruit Punch.
I prefer the berry blast over the fruit punch, but which flavor you like is completely up to you!
