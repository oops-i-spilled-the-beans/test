PIE PIE PIE PIE

Hello everyone, today I will be talking about why pie is so good!

Pie is good because it has a very yummy filling, along with a crunchy crust. Once you bite into it, you get a bit of both worlds, and the textures nad tastes work very well with each other.

My favorite type of pie is Pumpkin Pie, because I like how all of the spices mix well with the pumpkin and make it taste AWESOME!
